About Us

Piotr Swiatek

Clinical Audiologist & Hearing Aid Consultant

Piotr Swiatek has completed his training in Clinical Physiology & Audiology at The Middlesex University in London in 2009, follow by BSc in Hearing Science from Anglia Ruskin University in Cambridge. He also completed a BSc degree from the Open University, where he studied modules relevant to employment in health, social work or social care. This course helped him to develop an awareness and gain knowledge about formal and informal framework of health and social care, and of ethical, social, economic and political issues that influence delivery of Health Care services.

Piotr is a member of the Registered Council for Clinical Physiologists who governed the NHS audiologists. He is also the hearing aid dispenser, registered with the Health and Care Professions Council. He has a comprehensive knowledge in clinical physiology and audiology and hearing aids technology. He understands fully the physical and psychological basis of hearing and hearing disorders and the impact of hearing impairment on the individual’s life.

Piotr has a broad knowledge to perform audiological assessment and hearing aids fitting, his socials skills help him to identify and treat hearing impairment, tinnitus and other auditory disorders. He is currently working as a Team Leader of Audiology Services for an independent sector organisation, based in the South East providing Audiology Service at 11 clinic locations across Surrey, Sussex and Berkshire both for NHS patients.

Some patients reject hearing aids due to social stigma of hearing loss or are in a state of denial and thus try to hide it from others. Many people lose their confidence in themselves simply because they have a hearing loss. They believe others will think they are getting older less competent, less attractive etc. Piotr’s aim is to change this, by encouraging them to use their hearing aids.

Audiology Plus offers a comprehensive diagnostic hearing assessment for hearing loss, fitting of hearing aids, hearing aid aftercare for patients at the audiology clinic in Ashtead Hospital.

Contact us if you have any questions, concerns, or if you would like to schedule an appointment.
