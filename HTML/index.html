Audiology Plus offers a comprehensive diagnostic hearing assessment for hearing loss, fitting of hearing aids and hearing aid aftercare for patients.

Hearing assessments includes:

  • A full medical history (to understand, if possible, the cause of hearing deficit and to understand how the hearing deficit impacts on your everyday life.)
  • Otoscopy examination (involves looking into the ear in order to examine the external auditory canal.)
  • Tympanometry (to measure the size of the ear canal and to gage the middle ear pressure and compliance of the ear drum.)

* Any abnormalities detected will be referred directly to the patients’ GP or an ENT consultant. Audiology Plus provides a range of hearing aids suitable for the patient’s needs, noise protection ear plugs and swim plugs. We provide tinnitus counselling, and tinnitus maskers. We can help with listening and communication strategies, arrange assistive listening devices or refer to the Sensory Support Team for any further help. Audiology Plus can provide domiciliary visits on request.

Hearing impaired people are often subject to prejudice and misconception.

We strive to remove that stigma by providing personalized care, education and the latest technology to our patients in order to help improve their lives.
