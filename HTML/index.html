Meet Piotr Swiatek

Piotr Swiatek has a comprehensive knowledge in clinical physiology and audiology and hearing aids technology.

Piotr has completed his training in Clinical Physiology & Audiology at The Middlesex University in London in 2009, follow by BSc in Hearing Science from Anglia Ruskin University in Cambridge. He also completed a BSc degree from the Open University, where he studied modules relevant to employment in health, social work or social care.
